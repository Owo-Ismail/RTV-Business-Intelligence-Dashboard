# RTV (Real-Time Visibility) Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

The RTV (Real-Time Visibility) Dashboard is a Business Intelligence solution developed for an academic technology institution to provide management with real-time visibility into student enrollment performance, revenue generation, course profitability, and location performance.

The dashboard consolidates key operational and financial metrics into a single interactive reporting platform, enabling data-driven decision-making across the institution.

---

## Business Problem

Management required a centralized reporting solution to answer critical business questions:

- Which courses generate the most revenue?
- Which locations are performing best?
- How effectively are prospective students converting into registered students?
- Which programs require additional investment?
- What areas need improvement to increase enrollment and revenue?

Without a centralized dashboard, stakeholders relied on manual reporting processes that made it difficult to monitor performance trends and make timely decisions.

---

## Data Preparation

The project involved:

- Cleaning and validating source datasets.
- Handling missing and inconsistent records.
- Transforming raw data using Power Query.
- Appending tables using Power Query.
- Creating relationships between multiple tables.
- Building a scalable data model for reporting.

---

## Data Modelling

A relational data model was created to connect:

- Students
- Courses
- Locations
- Revenue Transactions
- Enrollment Records

The model supports efficient filtering, drill-through analysis, and KPI calculations.

## Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

The dashboard identifies which courses and locations generate the highest revenue, allowing management to prioritize investments in high-performing programs.

### Student Conversion

Conversion metrics reveal the effectiveness of student acquisition efforts and help identify opportunities to improve enrollment rates.

### Operational Efficiency

The dashboard highlights underperforming locations and courses that may require operational improvements or marketing support.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing courses.
- Expand successful programs to additional locations.
- Investigate causes of low conversion rates.
- Improve marketing efforts for underperforming courses.
- Monitor revenue trends regularly to support strategic planning.
